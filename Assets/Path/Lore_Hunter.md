# Lore Hunter
### Path


## Abilities:
- [ ] When you [Swear an Iron Vow](Moves/quest/swear_an_iron_vow) (formidable or greater) to recover valuable knowledge or an extraordinary relic, reroll any dice. When you [Reach a Milestone](Moves/quest/reach_a_milestone) in the pursuit of that quest, take +2 momentum. When you [Fulfill Your Vow](Moves/quest/fulfill_your_vow) and score a hit, also mark 2 ticks on your discoveries legacy track.

- [ ] When you make a move to conduct extended research or study, reroll any challenge dice. On a match, you piece together an extraordinary or harrowing new theory; envision the nature of this revelation and mark 1 tick on your discoveries legacy track.

- [ ] When you recall esoteric knowledge to [Secure an Advantage](Moves/adventure/secure_an_advantage) or [Gain Ground](Moves/combat/gain_ground), add +1. On a hit, envision the obscure but helpful fact, theory, or technique you put to use, and take +1 momentum.
